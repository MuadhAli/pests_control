Interior Inspection & Treatment

The following are some ways that you can expedite the extermination process:

  • Inspect interior wall joints, cracks, and crevices for pest infestation.
  • Create an interior barrier that repels pests.
  • Treat beneath sinks and other common entry or breeding areas.

Exterior Inspection & Treatment

  • Inspect exterior for entry points, migration paths, and harborage areas.
  • Create a 3 foot barrier around the entire home.
  • Sweep the eaves, overhangs, entryways, and windows for spider webs, egg sacs, and wasp nests.
  • Treat doorway and window joints.

Garage Inspection & Treatment

The following are some ways that you can expedite the extermination process:

  • Inspect wall joints, cracks, crevices, and rafters for pest infestation.
  • Sweep the walls, ceiling, and rafters for spiders, spider webs, and egg sacs.
  • Create an interior barrier that repels pests.

Yard Inspection & Treatment

The following are some ways that you can expedite the extermination process:

  • Inspect yard for signs of infestation and harborage areas.
  • Sweep play structures and sheds for spider webs, egg sacs, and remove wasp nests.
  • Treat cracks, crevices, and expansion joints in driveways and sidewalks.
  • Treat around bushes and shrubs.
  • Granulate flower beds to discourage nesting.
  • Spot treat the yard up to 30 feet from the home.

Perimeter Inspection & Treatment

  • Inspect perimeter for signs of infestation, migration paths, and harborage areas.
  • Sweep fences for spider webs, egg sacs, and wasp nests.
  • Create a property barrier to protect against your neighbors’ pests.
